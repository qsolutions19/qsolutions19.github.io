Our Research Focus

The Impact of COVID-19 on Educators


What Are We Researching?

Our team's research focus is centered on the impact of COVID-19 on educators.
We want to see how quarantine and remote learning has affected teachers' academic experience, across the country and all around the world.
How have their teaching methods changed?
How have their students' attendance and performance have changed?
How has their lifestyle changed?

Educators: We Need Your Help. Your Voice is Invaluable .
